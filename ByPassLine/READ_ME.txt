The by-pass line module is one of common modules of Ammonia Demonstrator. The components in the by-pass line module is controlled/report by the OouletPi

The By-pass line module consists of:
1. AC motor driver for Vaccuum creator


2. 3CH RS485 relay to control solenoid valves
https://wiki.diustou.com/en/RS485_Modbus_Relay

3. 2*RS485 ammonia sensor
